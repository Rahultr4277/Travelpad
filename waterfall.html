Munnar's Waterfalls


Munnar is one of the most beautiful places in the Southern part of India. It is also known as the tourist best and favourite destination for Holidays. It has beautiful and green tea gardens, hill stations, mountains, waterfalls, the amazing and world famous kathakali dance, globally recognised Ayurveda centers, and much more. Well, to make the place known to the visitors, we have picked the nature’s beauty Munnar’s Waterfalls. Now, let us tell you about the most attractive beauty of Munnar, i.e. its waterfalls. When we hear the word “waterfall”, we imagine the amazing flow of fresh water from the top of hills, mountains or a river. This is indeed nature’s blessed beauty. Well, to see this natural beauty of nature, Munnar is the best and one of the top places that is wrapped with jaw dropping waterfalls.
